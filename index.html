Welcome to Srinivasa Refrigeration Works!

At Srinivasa Refrigeration Works, we have been dedicated to providing top-notch services for air conditioners and refrigerators since our establishment in 1998. With over 25+ years of experience in the industry, we have earned a reputation for excellence and reliability in Tuni.

Our mission at Srinivasa Refrigeration Works is clear: we're dedicated to delivering exceptional repair, maintenance, and installation services for your cooling appliances. With a focus on customer satisfaction, we handle everything from minor repairs to complex installations with precision and efficiency. Our commitment to transparency and honesty ensures fair pricing and accurate assessments for all our services. Trusted by our valued customers for our expertise and personalized approach, you can rely on us for all your cooling needs, whether it's a sudden breakdown, routine maintenance, or system upgrades.

Thank you for choosing Srinivasa Refrigeration Works. We look forward to serving you and exceeding your expectations!
